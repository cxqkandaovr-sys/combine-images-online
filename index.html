Image Combiner FAQ

How do I combine two images into one?

Upload two photos, choose a side-by-side or vertical layout, then customize and download the result.

Can I combine more than two images?

Yes. You can upload and arrange up to 9 images in one layout.

Can I put images side by side or vertically?

Yes. Choose a horizontal, vertical, grid, or auto layout based on your images and intended use.

Can I add borders or a background color?

Yes. Adjust spacing, borders, corner radius, and background to match your design.

What image formats can I download?

Download your combined image as a PNG or JPG file.

Does this tool use AI to merge photos?

No. This tool arranges your uploaded images into one layout without generating new image content.
